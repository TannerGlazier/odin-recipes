Hamburger

Description

Hamburgers are a great choice for a summer cook-out by the pool!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices of cheese (optional)
  • Condiments (ketchup, mustard, lettuce, tomato, etc.)

Steps

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a bowl, mix ground beef with salt and pepper.
  3. Form into 4 patties.
  4. Grill patties for 5-7 minutes on each side.
  5. Add cheese slices during the last minute of cooking.
  6. Toast hamburger buns on the grill.
  7. Assemble burgers with condiments and toppings of your choice.
