Tacos

Description

Tacos are a delicious addition to any fiesta!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 8 taco shells
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • Sour cream (optional)
  • Hot sauce (optional)

Steps

  1. In a skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat.
  2. Drain excess fat and add taco seasoning according to package instructions.
  3. Warm taco shells in the oven or microwave.
  4. Spoon seasoned beef into taco shells.
  5. Add lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and any other desired toppings.
  6. Serve with sour cream and hot sauce if desired.
  7. Enjoy your delicious tacos!
