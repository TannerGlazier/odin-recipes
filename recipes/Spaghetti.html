Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti makes any meal feel fancy

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil (optional)
  • Olive oil (for cooking)

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add spaghetti and cook according to package instructions until al dente.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat and brown the ground beef.
  4. Drain excess fat and add marinara sauce to the skillet.
  5. Simmer for 5-10 minutes.
  6. Drain spaghetti and add it to the skillet with the sauce.
  7. Toss to combine and season with salt and pepper.
  8. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil if desired.
  9. Enjoy your delicious spaghetti!
